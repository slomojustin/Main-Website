## Required Audio Files

Place these files in the `/public/audio/` folder:

### 1. **background.mp3** (Background Music)
- **Type**: Ambient/Cinematic background music
- **Duration**: 2-5 minutes (will loop)
- **Volume**: Will play at 15% volume
- **Vibe**: Dark, tactical, atmospheric
- **Suggestions**:
  - Dark ambient music
  - Cinematic tension tracks
  - Lo-fi instrumental beats
  - Noir-style jazz

### 2. **hover.mp3** (Hover Sound Effect)
- **Type**: Short UI hover sound
- **Duration**: 0.1-0.3 seconds
- **Volume**: Will play at 30% volume
- **Vibe**: Subtle, tactical "target acquired" beep
- **Suggestions**:
  - Soft "tick" or "beep"
  - Subtle whoosh
  - Light click

### 3. **click.mp3** (Click Sound Effect)
- **Type**: Button click sound
- **Duration**: 0.1-0.3 seconds
- **Volume**: Will play at 30% volume
- **Vibe**: Tactical, satisfying click
- **Suggestions**:
  - Gun safety click
  - Tactical gear click
  - Satisfying "thunk"

### 4. **transition.mp3** (Optional - Page Transition)
- **Type**: Smooth transition sound
- **Duration**: 0.5-1 second
- **Volume**: Will play at 30% volume
- **Vibe**: Smooth, cinematic swipe

## Audio Controls

- **Mute Button**: Top right of header (next to theme toggle)
- **Auto-start**: Music starts automatically after loading screen
- **Interactive**: Buttons play sounds on hover and click
- **Browser-friendly**: Handles autoplay restrictions gracefully

## Testing Without Audio Files

The app will work even without audio files - it just won't play any sounds. No errors will be thrown.

## Tips

1. **Keep files small**: Compress audio to ~128kbps for faster loading
2. **Format**: MP3 is universally supported
3. **Volume**: Files are already volume-adjusted in code - just provide normalized audio
4. **Length**: Background music can be short (2-3 min) since it loops
